Est. 2020

Born from a Mother's Love

Cotto Mama began in a small studio in Mumbai, with a simple belief: children deserve clothes as joyful as they are — soft, colourful, and kind to the earth.

🌿

Organic Fabrics

All our fabrics are GOTS-certified organic cotton — breathable, soft, and free from harmful chemicals. Safe for your child's skin.

🎨

Designed for Joy

Every colour, print, and cut is designed to spark wonder. Our pieces encourage movement, creativity, and play.

💚

Sustainably Made

We work with ethical factories and aim for zero waste in our production process. Good for little ones, good for the planet.

Meet the Founder

👩

"When my daughter was born, I couldn't find clothes that were both beautiful and gentle on her sensitive skin. So I made my own — and Cotto Mama was born."

— Priya, Founder & Mama
